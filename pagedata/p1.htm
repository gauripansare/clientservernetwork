Client/Server Networks

The key learning objectives for this simulation are:

  • Identify the various types and characteristics of servers used on client/server networks.
  • Determine which servers are appropriate to use in given situations.

You are participating in the installation or expansion of a client/server network.

In this simulation, you get to decide which servers would best serve the needs of a company, based on descriptions of the company’s needs. You will apply what you’ve learned about servers.
